E64-Inductance of a coil with a finite number of turns in the presence of a magnetic material (mu_r>100)

The MATLAB function E64.m is a stand-alone accurate simulator for inductance of a coil with/without magnetic material. Its general features include:

  • User friendly and intuitive programmatic GUI
  • The project I/O data may be saved in separate project file(s)
  • Implemented entirely in basic MATLAB (R2011a or later)
  • No MATLAB toolboxes are needed

For the description of technical features, please see Sections 10.1 and 10.4 of Chapter 10 of the textbook
